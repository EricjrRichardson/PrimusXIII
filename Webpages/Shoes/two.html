Jordan 2

The Air Jordan 2, released in 1986, marked a departure from the bold design of its predecessor. Designed by Bruce Kilgore, it featured a luxurious Italian-made design with faux lizard skin accents. Despite lacking the same initial impact as the Jordan 1, the Jordan 2 remains a classic silhouette appreciated for its craftsmanship and elegance.
