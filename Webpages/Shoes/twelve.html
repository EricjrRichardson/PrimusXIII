Jordan 12

The Air Jordan 12, released in 1996, continued to push the boundaries of sneaker design and performance. Designed by Tinker Hatfield, it drew inspiration from the Japanese flag and featured a full-length Zoom Air unit for responsive cushioning. Michael Jordan won his fifth NBA championship while wearing the Jordan 12.
